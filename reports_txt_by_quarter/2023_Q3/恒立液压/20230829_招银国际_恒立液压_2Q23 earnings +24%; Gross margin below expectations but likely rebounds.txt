Valuation
We maintain our BUY rating on Hengli with new TP of RMB83 (previously: RMB90), based
on unchanged 42x 2023E P/E, equivalent to 1SD above the 4-year average of 30x. Our
above-average P/E is to reflect the acceleration of earnings growth driven by the overseas
expansion and new opportunities in robotic component (ball screw) segment.
