Xinhua Insurance Report mid-2018 Summary Report/Non-Bank Finance
On 28 August, Xinhua Insurance published its semi-annual report for 2018, which achieved business income of $83,669 million, or 8.5 per cent, and a net return of $5,799 million, or 79.1 per cent, to the mother.
Net profits increased by nearly 80 per cent, mainly for three reasons: 1) the company was in a period of transition in 2017, with a lower base of prior-period profits; 2) the effect of the transition in 2018 became apparent, with an improvement in the quality of life insurance operations; and 3) the reserve assessed interest rate adjustments for the first half of the year, resulting in a combined increase of pre-tax profits of $147 million.
