Public media (002027.sz): The strategic investment from Alibaba is more profitable than the long one, where companies invest about 15 billion yuan. The strategic investment will consist of three components: 1) two existing shares in the mass media, which will be donated to Ali's family at a price of 7.68 billion yuan; and 2) the shareholders in Giovannacayman and Gio2(hk) will be invited to pay their share.
100 per cent of the shares of the two companies held (2.71 per cent of the shares in the media as of July 2018) were to be transferred to the Ali family at $594 million ($3.9 billion in the RMB); 3) the Ali community was to contribute about $511 million ($3.4 billion in the RMB) to acquire the Mmmhl (currently held by the 100 per cent shareholding company of Jiang Namchun, the head of the media division.
By July 2018, mmhl held 23.34% of the shares in the mass media, while Alibaba also indicated that it would decide to hand over the bulk in the next 12 months, at a reasonable price, depending on the circumstances.
