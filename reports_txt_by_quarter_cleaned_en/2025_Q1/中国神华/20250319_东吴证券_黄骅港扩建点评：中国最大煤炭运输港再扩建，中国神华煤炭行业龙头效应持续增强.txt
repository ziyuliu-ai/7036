On 19 March 2025, China's largest coal transport port was built, and the Chinese coal industry's flagship effects continued to increase.
We learned from the National Energy Group that our largest coal transport port, the Hoang Hao Harbour, has again been expanded to include the fourth expansion of coal transport. This expansion is planned for an additional 47,000 tons of coal loading docks, 18 30,000 tons of silos and two coal dumps.
The annual storage capacity of coal will increase from 4.6 million tons to 5.14 million tons, up to 260 million tons.
