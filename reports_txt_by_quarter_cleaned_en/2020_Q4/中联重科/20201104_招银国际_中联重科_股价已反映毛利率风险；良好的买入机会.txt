In the third quarter, the decrease was due to: (a) an increase in sales of small car cranes and tower cranes with lower profitability rates; (b) a lower profit rate of concrete blending; faster growth of vehicles than concrete pumps; and (c) a rapidly developing high-altitude operating platform compared to existing operations
