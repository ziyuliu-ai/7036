Performance was significantly affected by the epidemic, with the creation of the generic pharmaceutical product cluster, which was declared production on 31 August 2020, providing strong support for corporate project R & D and innovative development.
The impact of the epidemic on the company’s net profits is significant, and new products are limited. The impact of the epidemic is significant, mainly due to the following factors: (a) the impact of the new coronary virus outbreak on the non-epidemiology of medical institutions, which has affected the treatment of non-epidemiological units to a certain extent, has led to a decline in the sale of the company’s fluids and non-revenue products.
(ii) Continued optimization of the Ishachuanin process and the full release of the production capacity of the subsidiary, resulting in a steady decline in the production costs of the product.
