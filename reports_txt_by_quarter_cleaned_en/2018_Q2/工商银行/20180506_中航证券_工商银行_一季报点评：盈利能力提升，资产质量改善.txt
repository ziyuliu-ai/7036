Net interest income increased by 12.33 per cent in the quarter over the same period, up from 9.67 percentage points in the previous year, representing an increase of 69.48 per cent in the collection ratio, an increase of 5.12 percentage points over the same period last year. Non-interest income decreased by 10.91 per cent in the same period.
The impact is a 39.89 per cent decline in the industry ' s income from other operations on a year-on-year basis, which is a drag on revenue performance.
