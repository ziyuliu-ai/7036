China Power (600482) 2017: The advantages of the internal resource integration engine platform are highlighted, and the reported performance is stable. The company’s net profit growth in the first half of the year was largely due to increased corporate income.
Gains on investment in property, increased off-the-shelf income and reduced sales and financial costs include an increase of 10.14 per cent in revenue on a year-on-year basis, a decrease of 2.87 per cent in sales costs as a result of improved quality of sales, and a decrease of 50.21 per cent in financial costs as a result of lower expenditure on dry interest.
The company benefits from the Marine Power strategy and naval transition and equipment upgrading needs, takes over the development and production of various types of naval power equipment in the country, and expands its military trade based on a “one-way” strategy, with revenues from the company’s military operations accounting for the first half of 2017.
To promote steady growth in corporate business by 18 per cent, the agreement provides for the establishment of China with 100 per cent equity financing from IYA-Tsai, China Heavy Industries Group and China Heavy Industries.
