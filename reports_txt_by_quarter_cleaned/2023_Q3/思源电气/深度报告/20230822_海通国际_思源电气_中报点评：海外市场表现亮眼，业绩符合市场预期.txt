Summary
The growth rate of revenue and net profit is in line with market expectations. On August 18th, Sieyuan Electric released its
1H2023 financial report, it achieved a revenue of 5.303 billion yuan in 1H2023, a year-on-year increase of 22.05%. In 2Q2023, it
achieved a revenue of 3.131 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 20.14%, and a quarter-on-quarter increase of 44.15%. the net
profit attributable to the parent company is 701 million yuan, a year-on-year increase of 48.08%. In 2Q2023, it achieved a net
profit attributable to the parent company of 182 million yuan, a year-on-year increase of 50.47%, and a quarter-on-quarter
increase of 121.6%.
From a business perspective, the growth rate of the reactive power compensation business is the fastest. The revenue of the
switch business was 2.628 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 9.2%, accounting for 49.56%. The revenue of the coil business
was 1.112 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 28.13%, accounting for 20.98%. The revenue of the reactive power compensation
business was 767 million yuan, a year-on-year increase of 80.25%, with the fastest growth rate, accounting for 14.46%. The revenue
of the smart devices business was 348 million yuan, with a growth rate of 0.36%, accounting for 6.55%.
From a regional perspective, overseas revenue exceeded 1 billion, with a year-on-year growth of 47.5%. The company's overseas
business revenue and proportion continue to increase, achieving revenue of 1.081 billion yuan, accounting for more than 20%,
and a comprehensive gross profit margin of 32.3%. The company has a wide distribution in markets such as Europe, Latin America,
Africa, and Southeast Asia, and multiple products have passed certification in more than 10 countries such as France, the United
Kingdom, Spain, and Portugal. It is expected to fully benefit from the demand for overseas power primary and secondary
equipment increment and replacement.
Due to the price of raw materials decreasing and the US dollar appreciation, the gross profit margin has significantly increased.
In 1H2023, the comprehensive gross profit margin was 29.06%, an increase of 3.46% compared to the same period last year.
Among them, the gross profit margin of the switch business was 31.46%, the coil business was 32.98%, the reactive power
compensation business was 22.06%, the intelligent equipment business was 30.39%, and the engineering general contracting was
11.21%, an increase of 5.74%, 3.15%, 1.34%, 1.32%, and 0.19% respectively compared to the same period last year. The main
reason is that the price of raw materials decreased significantly, and the positive impact of RMB depreciation.
The R&D intensity was high with the growth rate of R&D expenses has reached 60%, actively promoting a diversified product
layout. In addition to delving deeply into its traditional businesses, the company vigorously expands its business boundary, Sieyuan
electric actively exploring technological investments in flexible DC transmission, optoelectronic technology, energy storage,
Internet of Things, automotive electronics business, etc. It has acquired 19.22% equity in GMCC, laid out its business to
supercapacitors to continuously deepened the technical barriers.
Profit forecast and investment suggestions: Based on the order and earning growth rate guidances in 2023, we expect the
company's operating revenue to be 14.08/18.19/22.40 billion yuan from 2023 to 2025, respectively, and the net profit attributable
and the rating of "OUTPERFORM".
Risk: 1. The construction of ultra-high voltage lines and policies are not as expected; 2. The fierce market competition has led to a
significant decrease in gross profit margin; 3. The prices of raw materials have significantly increased; 4. Foreign exchange risk.
