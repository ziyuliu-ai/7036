热点速评 Flash Analysis
(Please see APPENDIX 1 for English summary)
看点 1: 北方稀土 2021 年实现营收 304.08 亿元，同增 38.83%；归母净利 51.30 亿元，同增 462.32%。
看点 2: 公司 21 年具备冶炼分离产能 12 万吨/年、稀土金属产能 1.6 万吨/年。稀土功能材料产品包括稀土磁性材
料、抛光材料、贮氢材料、催化材料。21 年稀土矿产品总量控制指标为 16.8 万吨，同增 20%，公司获得量占总量
指标下达量的 60%。
看点 3: 行业集中度有望继续提升。我国稀土配额逐年增加，北方稀土的占比始终高于 50%。包钢股份排他性提供
精矿,价格优势明显。
看点 4: 围绕“双碳”目标，催化氧化镨钕需求增加。以 21-25 年为例，据百川盈孚测算，1 辆新能源车的永磁电机需
1kg 氧化镨钕, 需求 CAGR 为 40%。另外, 风电领域的直驱/半直驱永磁电机带动氧化镨钕需求 CAGR 为 22%。高端
变频空调渗透率提升,带动氧化镨钕需求 CAGR 为 37%。
公司产能释放低于预期,新能源车销量低于预期。
Summary
Aspect 1: In 2021, Northern Rare Earth achieved a revenue of 30.408 billion yuan, experiencing an increase of 38.83% on a year-
over-year basis. The net profit attributable to the mother was 5.130 billion yuan, experiencing an increase of 462.32% on a year-
over-year basis.
Aspect 2: In 2021, the company's smelting and separation capacity was 120,000 tons/year, and the production capacity of rare
earth metals was 16,000 tons/year. Rare earth functional materials products include rare earth magnetic materials, polishing
materials, hydrogen storage materials and catalytic materials. In 2021, the total control index of rare earth mineral products was
168,000 tons, experiencing an increase of 20% on a year-over-year basis, and the company's acquisition accounted for 60% of the
total amount of the target.
Aspect 3: We believe that industry concentration will continue to increase. China's rare earth quota has increased year by year
and the proportion of Northern Rare Earth has always been higher than 50%. Inner Mongolia Baotou Steel Union exclusively
provides concentrates, and the price advantage is obvious.
Aspect 4: Around the "double carbon" target, the demand for catalytic praseodymium oxide is increasing. Taking 2021-2025 as an
example, it is estimated that the permanent magnet motor of a new energy vehicle will need 1kg of praseodymium oxide and the
compound annual growth rate of demand is 40%. In addition, the demand for neodymium praseodymium oxide driven by direct-
drive/semi-direct-drive permanent magnet motors in the wind power sector will be growing at a CAGR of 22%. The CAGR of
demand for neodymium praseodymium oxide driven by increased penetration of high-end inverter air conditioners is 37%.
Risks:
The company's production capacity release is lower than expected, and sales of new energy vehicles are lower than expected.
