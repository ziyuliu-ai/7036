Summary
Event:
Postal Savings Bank announces 2024 third quarter results. 24Q3 Revenue +0.5%, turning positive, profit before provision +8.8%,
net profit attributable to the parent +3.5%. 24Q1-3 revenue +0.1%, turning positive, pre-provision profit -1.8%, net profit
attributable to the parent +0.2%. The annualized ROA of 24Q1-3 was -0.07pct to 0.62%, and the annualized ROE was -1.11pct to
11.79%. CET1 capital ratio was -0.04pct to 9.42% YoY. At present, the A-share 2024E P/B is 0.6x, 2024E P/E is 6.0x, TTM dividend
yield is 5.0%, and the industry average is 0.6x, 5.4x, 4.8%.
Comments:
In Q3 2024, the decline in asset-side yield and liability-side cost rate declined by roughly the same margin.
\triangleright
Corporate loans were the main contributor to loan growth, with the growth rates for deposits and loans weaker than the
same period last year, aligning with industry trends.
\triangleright
NPL ratio, special mention loan ratio, and overdue loan ratio all increased QoQ.
\triangleright
\triangleright
The 24Q3 cost-to-income ratio was 60.0%, +0.8% YoY.
Risk: Asset quality may underperform expectations.
