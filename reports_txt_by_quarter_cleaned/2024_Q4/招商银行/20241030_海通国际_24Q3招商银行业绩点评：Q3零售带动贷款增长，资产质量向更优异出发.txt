attributable to parent +0.8% YoY. 24Q1-3 Revenue -2.9%, profit before provision -2.7%, net profit attributable to the parent -0.6%.

to 14.73% YoY. At present, the 2024E P/B is 0.9x, 2024E P/E is 6.6x, TTM dividend yield is 5.1%, and the industry average is 0.6x,
5.5x, 4.7%.
